Welcome to our Buguer Shop

Handcrafted and homespun are just the beginning. An appetite for over-the-top creative expression and innovation is precisely what put America on the map, and for 30 years and counting, Burguer Shop, too. Smiling, singing and dancing are always in season, regardless of decade or DJ. By turning our guests on to a better way to do today – every day – through savory food and a side of fun gives us all the opportunity to do it again tomorrow.

Trends and tastes change, but the human condition to connect doesn’t. At Burguer Shop, our menus offer comfort and our customer service continually delivers smiles. Johnny Burguer Shop is more than a restaurant, it’s an escape-from-the-ordinary experience that brings every day – and everyone – an excuse to celebrate, making memories and meals that live beyond the booth. Our passion for our freshly-made, classic burgers and indulgent, hand-spun real ice cream shakes, is only equaled by our commitment to a superb guest experience.

About Us

Founded on the belief that everyone deserves a place where they can escape from the complicated world, Burguer Shop opened its first restaurant on June 6th, 1986, on the iconic Melrose Ave. in Los Angeles, California. Today, our timeless All-American brand connects with customers across the U.S. and around the globe where we operate restaurants in 25 countries. At Burguer Shop we strive to make every guest smile every day with irresistible food and our passion for fun.

Every day, more than 11 million guests visit Burguer Shop restaurants around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are known for serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 1954, Burguer Shop is the second largest fast food hamburger chain in the world. The original Home of the Whopper, our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 50 successful years.
